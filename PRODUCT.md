# Nova Store — Product Context

## What we are

Nova Store is a B2C e-commerce storefront for undetected game enhancements (aimbot, ESP, spoofers, etc.). Checkout is powered by Sellauth embed. Bilingual Arabic (primary) and English.

## Audience

- Competitive gamers (18–35) in MENA and international markets
- Buyers who value trust signals: undetected status, reviews, Discord support
- Mobile and desktop shoppers; RTL-first for Arabic

## Brand tone

- **Trust + power + professionalism** — not playful or childish
- Confident, direct copy; avoid hype spam
- Dark premium gaming aesthetic — red accent on near-black surfaces

## Strategic principles

1. Status and safety (undetected) are primary conversion drivers
2. Product imagery and game recognition beat generic stock photos
3. Fast path to purchase: store → product → buy
4. Discord community is the support hub

## Anti-references (do NOT emulate)

- Purple-to-blue SaaS gradients on dark mesh
- Generic 3 equal feature cards with emoji icons
- Inter + slate-900 default AI landing pages
- Centered hero with fake metrics ("10M+ users")
- Glassmorphism on every surface
- Bounce easing on UI transitions

## Register

**Brand** — marketing and store surfaces. Design IS the product experience.
